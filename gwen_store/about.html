About GWEN

GWEN is a small eyewear brand focused on minimal design, comfort and quality. Each frame is tested for durability, with anti-reflective and UV protection options.

We believe in slow design — fewer styles done exceptionally well. Our frames are crafted from premium acetate and lightweight metals, and every purchase ships with a protective case.

Our Values

  • Quality materials
  • Sustainable packaging
  • Transparent pricing

meet the GWEN(s)

Our GWEN Advisory Board is comprised of renowned experts in optometry and design. Guided by the latest research in eye health and fashion trends, they shape our eyewear innovation from initial concept to the final frame.
